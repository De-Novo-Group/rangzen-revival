üìª Radio Settings (Advanced)

Murmur uses multiple wireless radios simultaneously. You can see their status in the feed header and toggle them in Settings ‚Üí Radios:

  • Bluetooth (BLE) ‚Äî Always-on background discovery. Range ~10m, low power. Most reliable for finding peers.
  • WiFi Direct ‚Äî Longer range (~100m), faster transfers. May show a "connection invitation" popup on some devices.
  • WiFi Aware (NAN) ‚Äî Modern alternative to WiFi Direct. Same range/speed, no popups. Requires Android 8+ and supported hardware (not all devices have it).
  • LAN ‚Äî Works when devices share the same WiFi network. Fastest transfer speed but limited to local network.

Known limitations:

  • WiFi Direct popups can be intrusive on some Samsung devices
  • WiFi Aware is not available on older or budget phones
  • LAN only works if both devices are on the same WiFi
  • Some devices have aggressive battery optimization that kills background radios

Do NOT disable any radios unless specifically asked by the De Novo Group testing team. We need all radios active to test the full mesh behavior.
