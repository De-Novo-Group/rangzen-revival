üîß Troubleshooting

"No peers nearby"

  • Is "Enable Murmur" ON?
  • Is Bluetooth ON?
  • Is Location Permission granted?
  • Within range? (BLE ~10m, Wi-Fi ~50m)

"Messages not arriving"

  • Be patient ‚Äî discovery runs periodically
  • Pull-to-refresh to force scan

"App crashes"

  • Try: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Murmur ‚Üí Clear Data
  • May be needed if database schema changed
